Rimsha Enterprises Pvt Ltd. is situated at Thansing, ward no 1,Nuwakot.This is an Hardware Selling store which sells different items like building materials, hand tools, power tools, keys, locks, hinges, chains, plumbing supplies etc since last two years.
This organization was established  in  a year 2073 with the motive of delivering different hardware items covering the Nuwakot district for the quick and easy reconstruction purpose for the nearby people after the earthquake strikes in 2072.
This organization is one of the best in local areas with reasonable price and quality services. The best part is it also provides home delivery. Now it aims to digitalize the organization, and feels need of a computer system.
